MR.AYERS


Greek

    Mr. Ayers, your classes have always been some of the most stimulating, enjoyable classes I could have had. Having been taught by you for almost every year I was at Ridgeview, you have taught me unforgettable life lessons that I will keep in mind throughout my time in college. Having been able to be a part of such a well-taught and productive Greek class has given me a sense of pride I could not have attained anywhere else in the Ridgeview curriculum. I sincerely hope that you continue to have strong, educated opinions and always argue with your (more belligerent) students the same way you did with me.

Thank you for the unforgettable moments and classes. So long, and thanks for all the fish.

~ Hazel Daigneault ~
